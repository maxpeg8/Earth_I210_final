Ariel

Ariel is the brightest and reflective of Uranus's major moons and has a surface with fault systems and canyons. Ariel's surface appears to have both tectonic activity and cryovolcanism, which is when a moon or planet has an eruption of water, methane, or ammonia instead of molten rock. It is the fourth largest moon orbiting its planet and was discovered in 1851 with Umbriel.
